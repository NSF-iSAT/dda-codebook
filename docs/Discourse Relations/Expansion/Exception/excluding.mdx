## Definition

**Used when:** 

i.e. *This* is excluding *That*

## Examples

```
(1) A: Dolphins can't breath underwater.
(2) B: A lot of other sea creatures can.
---
(2) --Excluding--> (1)
```
the natural reading of the DDA edge: “a lot of other sea creatures can (breath under water)” excluding “dolphins (can’t breath underwater)”.
