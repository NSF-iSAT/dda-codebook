## Definition

**Used when:** arguments are presented as exclusive alternatives, with one being ruled out explicitly.

### Subtypes
- [Substituting](./substituting.mdx)
- [SubstitutedBy](./substitutedby.mdx)

### Examples

```
(1) A: We should do X and Y.
(2) B: Let's do Z instead.
---
(2) -- Substituting --> (1)
```
B suggests an alternative Z to X and Y that should replace them explicitly.
