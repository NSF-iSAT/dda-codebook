## Definition

**Used when:** one argument describes a situation as holding in a set of circumstances(or general description of a situation), while the other argument describes one or more of those circumstances(typically concrete instances of the circumstances).

### Subtypes
- [Instantiating](./instantiating.mdx)
- [InstantiatedBy](./instantiatedby.mdx)

### Examples

```
(1) A: foreign companies such as Givaudan that deal with those industries are being hit the hardest
(2) A: But in general, all foreign-trading companies are feeling the pinch.
---
(2) -- InstantiatedBy --> (1)
```

The circumstances "all foreign-trading companies are feeling the pinch" are instantiated with a particular company "Givaudan".
