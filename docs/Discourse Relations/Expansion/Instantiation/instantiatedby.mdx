## Definition

**Used when:** the source argument describes a situation as holding in a set of circumstances, while the target argument describes an instance of one or more of those circumstances.

i.e. *This* is instantiated by *That*

## Examples

```
(1) A: foreign companies such as Givaudan that deal with those industries are being hit the hardest
(2) A: But in general, all foreign-trading companies are feeling the pinch.
---
(2) -- InstantiatedBy --> (1)
```
