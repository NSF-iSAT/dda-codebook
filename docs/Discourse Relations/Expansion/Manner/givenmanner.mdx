## Definition

**Used when:** the source argument describes a situation for which the manner is provided in the target argument.

i.e. *This* is the manner given for *That*

## Examples

```
(1) A: I clicked the 'delete' button.
(2) A: To get rid of that.
---
(2) -- GivenManner --> (1)
```
(2) describes an outcome or situation (getting rid of something)achieved through the manner given in (1) (clicking the delete button).
