## Definition

**Used when:** the source argument provides the cause, reason, explanation or justification, while the target gives its effect.

i.e. *This* is causing *That*

## Examples

```
Example 1:
(1) A: I think you have to discard a card
(2) B: Ah I have too many
---
(2) --Causing--> (1)
```
