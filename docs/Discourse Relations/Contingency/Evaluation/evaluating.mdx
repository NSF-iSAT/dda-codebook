## Definition

**Used when:** in the source argument, the speaker communicates a positive or negative evaluation of the situation or utterance in the target argument.

i.e. *This* is evaluating *That*

## Examples

```
(1) Student: We just finished.
(2) Teacher: Awesome.
---
(2) -- Evaluating --> (1)
```
The speaker communicates that they regard that the student has finished positively.
The speaker is *evaluating* the student's completion of some task.
