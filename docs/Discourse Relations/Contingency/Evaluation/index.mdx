## Definition

The Evaluation relation involves assessing nuclear material in terms of **some frame of reference** that is not part of the subject matter of the nucleus itself.
Evaluation reflects positive or negative regard on the part of the writer/speaker;
The regard of the speaker has sentiment values in them.
