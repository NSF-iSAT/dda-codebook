## Definition

**Used when:** in the source argument presents a situation the speaker communicates a positive or negative evaluation of the situation or utterance in the target argument.

i.e. *This* is evaluating *That*

## Examples

```
(1) Teacher: That's awesome!
(2) Teacher: that you finished the project.
---
(2) -- EvaluatedBy --> (1)
```
The speaker communicates that they regard that the student has finished the project positively.
The speaker clarifies the situation (finishing the project) which is *evaluated by* their previous utterance.
