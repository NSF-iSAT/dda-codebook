## Definition

Used when: the events or situations in the two arguments are temporally ordered. If the precise order is known, 
instead use the sub-types [Before](./before.mdx) or [After](./after.mdx).

## Examples

```
Example 1:
(1) A: I won't be available from 3-4pm, I have a meeting at that time 
(2) B: Ok, I'll reach at a different time then
(2) --Asynchronous--> (1)
```
