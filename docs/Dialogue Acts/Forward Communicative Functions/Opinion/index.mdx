## Definition

**Used when:** the source argument is a personal opinion, belief, or judgement.
These differ from personal [Statements](../Statement) in that they are subjective and the listener could have their own, possibly differing opinion.

## Examples

```
(40) T: Yes, this parts easy
---
(40) -- Opinion --> (40)
```
T is expressing a personal opinion about the difficulty of a part of a task, which other participants might agree or disagree with.

```
(69) A: The editor looks bizarre.
---
(69) -- Opinion --> (69)
```
A is sharing a personal opinion about the appearance of an editor, which others may or may not share.
